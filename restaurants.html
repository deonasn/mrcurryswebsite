Mr. Currys - Foods & Services

RESTAURANTS


Deliciously Different, Healthy, Authentic & Traditional - Mr. Currys India Restaurant recipes are based on over 100 years old family recipes and traditions. We believe the Authenticity and Traditions are very important for any ethnic food and have done everything possible to maintain them. We have tried to eliminate all the misconceptions about Indian food and to maintain the pure traditions, which were followed for generations. We believe that is why we are able to attract large percentage of customers from 'all walk of life' to try our deliciously different food, and able to make them fall in love with Indian food, the flavors, healthiness, taste, & traditions.
